Venus

THE TOOL OF THE TRADE.

Do you really play your sport with the first things you have around? Leave assemblies and adjustments to the others.
Let us present you our Victrix Venus, the first European rifle designed for dynamic precision long range shooting, ready
for delivery and in different versions. Leave the unexpected to your competitors.

THE CHARM OF PRECISION.

Precision shooting needed beauty, to emphasize the harmony of the
sporting gesture. For this reason, Victrix Venus is born, our tribute to the
Venus victrix, the goddess who conquered the hearts of all people.
Venus is the sporting rifle of Victrix Armaments that celebrates the
charm of precision. Move, target, hit, repeat. Hit the target, shot after
shot, and do it again.

A VENUS FOR PRS.

We do not live on myths and legends. They inspire us; however, it is not
our reality. As we have decided to not live on dreams, but on work,
design and precision.
This means, for you, a catalogue rifle ready for competition, no wait for a
customization, no heavy unnecessary elements, a monobloc chassis
with an integrated Arca rail, and many other things. Beauty, essence,
precision: that is your new Venus.

PRECISION RIFLE SHOOTERS.

We are shooters, before being manufacturers and designers. What does this mean? Any choice
comes from our direct experience, and from the one of our experienced shooters, gained through
years. In this way, we can be innovators, to deliver you a perfect rifle and ready for the firing line.
