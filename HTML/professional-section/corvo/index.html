Corvo

A SNIPER RIFLE, IN .50 BMG.

We’ve designed Corvo to have more control over the extreme power, holding our accuracy
flag high. To give you an idea, think about the accuracy of a sniper firearm system together
with the energy of a .50 BMG caliber.

ONE-WAY.

With Victrix Corvo, the energy is released on the target, not on the
shooter. The strengths are managed aiming at the best control, thanks
to the mechanical couplings and our high-level of precision machining
stated by our production process.
In short: no recoil.

AN UNFLAPPABLE POWER.

We’ve designed our Victrix Corvo, as we wanted to make an important
caliber like the .50 BMG steady and available, without any of the
disadvantages typical of the .50 BMG. We just have to tame the energy
with accuracy. Should we say it again? No recoil.

HAND LUGGAGE.

17.000 joules of energy, in just over a meter. That’s the length of the case carrying a Victrix Corvo. It’s
something already well-known on the airplanes and special means of transport of different Italian and
international military forces.
