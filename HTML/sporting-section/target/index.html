BIG BANG THEORY.

The whole story of the Sporting Victrix starts here.
A project of Giuseppe Valtorta, conceived between his desk and the line
of fire, and developed by our engineering department working on the
machines of his Rottigni Officina Meccanica.

A WINNING COMBO.

We don’t care about making things out of habit.
We find it more challenging to search for new ways, explore new
boundaries. Our Victrix rifles of the Sporting series have Anima®, a
chassis with a light-alloy bedded monobloc, a metallic monolith installed
inside the wooden stock. You will have a modular firearm system, able
to adjuste to your targets.

Victrix Armaments provides you with everything you need to live the competitive sport shooting world
as protagonists, not spectators.
