Absolute

8 AM. CLASS IS OPEN.

With Victrix Absolute let’s open the chapter 1 of our book. Precision, Feeling, Stability. We installed a plate
in special alloy in the forend of our stock, on the forefront in contact with the rest. Why? To balance
the weight and reaction, and in the meantime to stiffen and gain more accuracy and feeling while shooting.

A PERFECT CLASSMATE.

We have installed the Anima® system also in the Victrix Absolute, our
stock with the monobloc bedding system in light alloy. A reliable
classmate, above all getting on well with all your targets. Anima® will
give your firearm system a modularity never seen.

THE KIDS ARE ALRIGHT.

How is a complex project like the Victrix Absolute rifle born? In the only
way possible, inspired by the best. We were on the line of fire together
with the most famous shooters: listening to, looking at them and
studying in detail any advice.
Next time, if you wish, you will know them.

WIN, OUT OF THE BOX.

That’s the formula to learn by heart. All calibers used in major top-level competitions are available,
ready to be delivered, at your trusted gun shop, and next minute, ready to shoot.
