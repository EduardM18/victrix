Scepter

ROYAL FAMILY.

Let us introduce you to Scepter V, the newcomer in Victrix Armaments ELR royal family. Have
a sit on the throne, wear your crown. The scepter you were looking for has come.

PRAISE TO DISTANCE.

Load, target, breathe. Enjoy shooting as never before. Choose among
the calibers available for Scepter V, .338 Lapua Magnum (1/9), .300
Norma Magnum (1/9,5) and .33 XC (1/9), compete over any distance,
including ELR distances. Your best shot is at hand.

A NEW BUTTSTOCK.

Find your best position to reach the target without all those annoying
movements. The slider is adjustable by means of a ring easy to get to., to
give the chance to adjust the buttstock height of your Scepter. So that
you can better concentrate when shooting, in absolute stability.

KEEP CONTROL.

As for other rifles of our Sporting series, also the chassis of Scepter is a monobloc chassis in
aluminium designed to increase stiffness, bending and torsion rigidity. The reticular structure
guarantees a consistent and controlled performance, shot after shot.
