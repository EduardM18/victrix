Gladio

FIELD-TESTED, FROM 2400 YEARS.

From the last legionary to the most important centurion, all of them had a Gladio. Our roots
lie in the history of a state that become an empire. The Victrix Gladio is our tribute to the
main weapon of the ancient Rome.

RIGHT STUFF.

We are creators of precision, it’s true. But we couldn’t stop there when
we designed our Victrix Gladio. We wanted a firearm modular system,
very strong and above all ergonomic.

CHOSEN BY SHOOTERS.

Our Victrix Gladio is the perfect rifle for a police sniper. Easy to handle,
ready to carry, it guarantees the best control over the strengths at work.
1000 meters are right behind the corner.

THE EVOLUTION OF SNIPING.

We have studied different scenarios and distances where you could find at work. For this reason, we
thought to offer you from the .308 Winchester caliber to .260 Remington, going through 6,5
Creedmoor.
